  - **link:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.19406
  - **Simple LLM Summary:** The paper critiques the classic Ethernet efficiency model by Metcalfe and Boggs for only measuring forward throughput, proposing instead a bilateral transaction efficiency that accounts for committed agreements between sender and receiver.
